About Kessa Integrated Solution

Kessa Integrated Solution is an integrated engineering service provider delivering solutions in electrical engineering, renewable energy consultation, energy management, and biomedical engineering support.

The company provides electrical wiring works, installation of electrical equipment, and consultation for solar photovoltaic (PV) systems for residential and commercial premises.

KESSA also provides energy management and energy audit services to help facilities improve energy efficiency and optimize operational costs.

In the healthcare sector, KESSA specializes in biomedical and low voltage equipment support with more than 10 years of technical experience in medical equipment asset management, preventive maintenance scheduling, and breakdown management for clinics and hospitals.

Biomedical services are performed in accordance with ISO 13485 quality management principles and SIRIM regulatory guidelines, with experience supporting government hospitals and healthcare facilities.
